Bespoke Design / UX project

Client: Hallmark CE (continental europe)

Duration: 8 weeks

The assigment of project was to design a prototype of app for card personalization tool and digital unwrapping.
Me and my design partnet have been doing this project following the Design Thinking process, which include:

  • Empathize (interview and observation).
  • Define (creating the Persona and draft Design Vision).
  • Ideate (HMW questions, braindump, diverging and converging tehniques).
  • Prototype (creating the storuboard, paper and digital prototype - Adobe XD).
  • Test and expo.

    • Me and my design partner been choosen for presenting our project to Hallmark during the expo.
